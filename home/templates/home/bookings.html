11 Plus Paper class (year6)

For those of you whose child is going into year 6 and is taking their 11 plus exam in the following September and October, we have paper classes running throughout August and September. We provide past papers as well as feedback for them from our tutors, all of whom have been Wilson's Grammar School or Wallington Grammar School themselves (see meet the team). We run 2 hour sessions, one hour for maths and one hour for english and charge an hourly rate of £10.

11 Plus Tutoring Sessions (year 5)

For those of you whose child is going into year 5 and is taking their 11 plus exam next year in 2018, we provide tuition classes which run on a weekly basis throughout the year. We will have 2 hour sessions (one hour for maths and one hour for english) at an hourly rate of £10 during which we will teach essential topics on the KS2 syllabus and how to tackle them. We provide ample homework for each child which we expect them to complete for the following week. Each homework pack will be different for each child, designed to target specific areas that they are struggling on specifically.
